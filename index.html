About SMYMKANJIRAPPALLY

The Syro-Malabar Youth Movement (SMYM) in the Eparchy of Kanjirappally is the official youth wing of the Syro-Malabar Catholic Church.

Formed to drive the holistic and spiritual development of young Catholics, it operates under the patronage of the Catholic Diocese of Kanjirapally, currently led by Bishop Mar Jose Pulickal. The diocese's geographic spread spans across 148 parishes in the civil districts of Kottayam, Idukki, and Pathanamthitta in Kerala, India.

Our Journey

A Legacy of Faith & Action

1978

Yuvaddepthy - KCYM Roots

The youth movement originally functioned under the name Yuvaddepthy - KCYM (Kerala Catholic Youth Movement), becoming active in the Diocese of Kanjirapally to drive holistic youth development.

1986

State-wide Peace Rallies

KCYM conducts four historic regional rallies starting from Kanjirapally, Kollam, Kodungalloor, and Thrissur during the World Peace Year.

2011

Global Unification Initiatives

The Syro-Malabar Laity Commission initiates efforts to unify various youth ministries across the globe under one banner.

2014

Official SMYM Launch

On August 30, 2014, the Synod of Bishops approves and officially launches the unified global Syro-Malabar Youth Movement (SMYM) structure.

2022

Unified Integration

KCYM Senate meetings officially incorporate SMYM and other rite-based movements into a coordinated state federation structure.

What We Do

Core Activities

A movement of action, prayer, and transformation.

Campus Ministry

Faith-based outreach, Bible study, and Catholic identity formation on college campuses.

Anti-Abuse Workshops

Runs institutional anti-substance abuse workshops on local campuses to empower youth.

De-Addiction Hubs

Sets up functional de-addiction hubs and safe peer support recovery circles.

Youth Camps

Residential leadership and spiritual formation camps, including the flagship Camp Insight.

Leadership Training

Structured programs to develop confident, Christ-centered leaders for Church and society.

Humanitarian Relief

Organizes direct community assistance during crises, raising relief funds and deploying food shipments (like bread and milk packs) to flood-affected communities like Chellanam.

Prayer Meetings

Regular gatherings for communal prayer, scripture reflection, and Eucharistic adoration.

Serve & Smash

Promotes youth interaction and physical health by hosting events, including the diocesan "Serve & Smash" volleyball tournament.
